About Our School

Founded in 2010, Frontiers Language School has established itself as a leader in bilingual education, combining international standards with local cultural values.

Our Mission

To develop globally-minded, multilingual learners who are prepared to meet the challenges of an interconnected world through innovative education that values diversity, critical thinking, and personal growth.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that empowers students to achieve academic excellence, cultural understanding, and responsible global citizenship through a dynamic multilingual curriculum.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Commitment to the highest standards in teaching and learning
  • Integrity: Fostering honesty, respect, and ethical behavior
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and forward-thinking approaches
  • Community: Building strong relationships among students, staff, and families
  • Diversity: Celebrating and learning from our multicultural environment

Academic Programs

Our comprehensive curriculum combines national standards with international best practices, offering a balanced approach to education that develops the whole child.

Kindergarten Program (KG1-KG2)

Our play-based kindergarten program focuses on developing social, emotional, cognitive and physical skills through hands-on activities in both English and Arabic.

  • Language development in English and Arabic
  • Early math and science concepts
  • Creative arts and music
Elementary School (Grades 1-6)

Building strong foundations in core subjects while encouraging curiosity and independent thinking.

  • English Language
  • Arabic Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Computer Science
  • French ( grade 2 )/German ( grade 3 )
Middle School (Grades 7-9)

Challenging academic program that prepares students for the rigors of high school while supporting their social and emotional development.

  • Advanced English and Arabic
  • Algebra and Geometry
  • Chemistry, Physics and Biology Sciences
  • World History and Geography
  • Second Foreign Language (French/German)
  • Art and Computer Technology ( ICT )
High School (Grades 10-12)

College-preparatory program offering Advanced Placement courses and comprehensive university counseling.

  • AP English Literature and Composition
  • AP Calculus and Statistics
  • AP Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
  • AP World History and Economics
  • Advanced Foreign Languages
  • University Preparation Program

Terms and Conditions

Admission Policy

Admission to Frontiers Language School is based on availability and the student's ability to meet the academic requirements of the program. The school reserves the right to accept or reject any application at its sole discretion.

Tuition and Fees

All tuition and fees are due according to the payment schedule provided at registration. Late payments may incur additional charges. Refunds are subject to the school's refund policy.

Code of Conduct

Students are expected to adhere to the school's code of conduct, which promotes respect, responsibility, and academic integrity. Violations may result in disciplinary action.

Privacy Policy

The school collects and uses student information in accordance with applicable privacy laws. Information may be used for educational purposes, administrative functions, and to maintain student safety.

Health and Safety

Parents must inform the school of any health conditions or allergies. The school will take reasonable precautions to ensure student safety but cannot guarantee a risk-free environment.
